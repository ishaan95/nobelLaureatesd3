Nobel Laureates

The nobel prize is the most prestigious award given in recognition of academic, cultural and scientific advancements. Purpose of this visualization is to educate people about the biggest advancements for humanity in the fields of literature, economics, medicine and the sciences. It is also immensely inspirational for young students to read about the greatest achievers and their discoveries.
